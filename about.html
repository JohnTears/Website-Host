Our Story

From humble beginnings to a Nyamitanga favorite

The Onesmus Restaurant Journey

How we became Nsikye's favorite local eatery

2010: Humble Beginnings

Onesmus started as a street vendor near Nyamitanga Market, selling Rolex and mandazi from a small wooden cart. His delicious food quickly gained popularity among market goers.

2013: First Restaurant

With savings from his street vending, Onesmus rented a small space in Nsikye and opened his first proper restaurant with just 4 tables. The menu expanded to include kikomando and katogo.

2017: Expansion

The restaurant moved to its current larger location, adding outdoor seating and a proper kitchen. This allowed for an expanded menu including daily specials.

2020: Community Recognition

Onesmus Restaurant was voted "Best Local Eatery" in Mbarara by the community. We began hosting weekly cultural nights with live music.

2023: Present Day

Now a Nyamitanga institution, we continue serving authentic Ugandan street food while training local youth in culinary skills. Our original Rolex recipe remains unchanged.

Our Food Philosophy

At Onesmus Restaurant, we believe in three simple principles:

Fresh Ingredients

We source our ingredients daily from Nyamitanga Market, supporting local farmers and ensuring peak freshness.

Traditional Recipes

Our dishes are prepared using time-honored methods passed down through generations.

Made with Love

Every dish is prepared with the same care and attention we would give our own family.

Giving Back to Nyamitanga

We believe in supporting the community that has supported us. Here's how we give back:

  • Sponsoring local students through vocational training programs
  • Weekly free meals for elderly community members
  • Annual "Taste of Mbarara" festival to promote local businesses
  • Donating unsold food to local orphanages each evening
