Meet Onesmus

"For over 10 years, I've been serving the Nyamitanga community with authentic Ugandan street food made with love and tradition."

Born and raised in Mbarara, Onesmus started his culinary journey as a street vendor before establishing this beloved local restaurant. His Rolex is legendary in Nsikye!
