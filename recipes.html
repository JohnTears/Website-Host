The Perfect Rolex

Learn to make our signature Rolex just like we do in the restaurant with this step-by-step guide.

Serves 2
450 cal/serving

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups wheat flour
  • 1 cup water
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 tomato, sliced
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 1/2 cabbage, shredded
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for frying

Watch How It's Made:

Instructions:

  1. Mix flour, water and salt to make a smooth dough
  2. Divide into 2 balls and roll out into thin circles
  3. Heat a pan and cook each chapati until golden brown
  4. In a separate pan, scramble the eggs with vegetables
  5. Place the egg mixture on the chapati and roll tightly
  6. Serve hot with chili sauce

Classic Kikomando

The ultimate quick breakfast that fuels Uganda - chapati and beans done right.

Serves 1
380 cal/serving

Ingredients:

  • 1 chapati (store-bought or homemade)
  • 1 cup cooked beans
  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • 1/2 onion, chopped
  • Salt to taste
  • Optional: avocado slices

Watch How It's Made:

Instructions:

  1. Heat oil in a pan and saut√© onions until soft
  2. Add cooked beans and mash slightly
  3. Season with salt and cook for 5 minutes
  4. Chop chapati into small pieces
  5. Mix chapati pieces with the bean mixture
  6. Serve hot with avocado if desired
